Thomas GW Electrician is an electrical company based in Telford. We specialize in domestic electrical service. We carry out all electrical works from changing lamps to full rewiring or replacement distribution boards or fast electrical troubleshouting. Registered as DOMESTIC INSTALLER in NICEIC we deliver electrical service on high quality, safe and in good workmanship. We are small company but very mobile with fast service and available for clients 24 hours 7 days a week. We can go in every place in the UK but most works we make in West Midland.
